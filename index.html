Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum ticket size you process?

We specialize in loan requirements starting from ₹25 Lakhs up to ₹500 Crores for corporate syndication.

Do you charge an upfront fee?

We work on a success-fee model for most structured finance deals, ensuring our interests are aligned with yours.

How long does the process take?

For unsecured business loans, 3-7 days. For complex project finance or secured loans, 2-4 weeks depending on documentation readiness.
